Nitrogen Fertilizers

Nitrogen is essential for leaf growth and overall plant development. It's a key component of chlorophyll and amino acids.

Benefits:

  • Promotes vegetative growth
  • Enhances leaf development
  • Improves protein content
  • Increases yield potential

Phosphorus Fertilizers

Phosphorus is crucial for root development, flowering, and fruit production. It helps plants store and transfer energy.

Benefits:

  • Strengthens root systems
  • Promotes flowering and fruiting
  • Enhances seed development
  • Improves disease resistance

Potassium Fertilizers

Potassium regulates water uptake and activates enzymes. It's vital for overall plant health and stress resistance.

Benefits:

  • Regulates water balance
  • Improves stress tolerance
  • Enhances fruit quality
  • Strengthens plant structure

Organic Fertilizers

Organic fertilizers improve soil structure and provide slow-release nutrients. They enhance soil microbial activity.

Benefits:

  • Improves soil health
  • Provides slow-release nutrients
  • Enhances microbial activity
  • Sustainable and eco-friendly
